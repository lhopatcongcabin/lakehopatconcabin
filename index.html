About the space

The home sits up 68 steps, so it is not ideal for guests who dislike stairs. Once you are up there, you get an elevated lake view and a comfortable stay with modern essentials.

Prepare meals in the kitchen or on the grill located on the top deck overlooking Lake Hopatcong. Downstairs there are two bedrooms, each with a queen bed. There is also a futon in the main living area.

Amenities

  • Central AC

  • Boat slip available for boats 25 ft or less with prior approval

Guest access

Guests have use of the entire home. Dock space and the lower lakeside area are shared with the owner, who utilizes the detached garage and does not reside onsite.
